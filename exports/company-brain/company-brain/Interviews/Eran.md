- The meeting involved discussions about procurement processes at Shufersal.
- Eran and his team are exploring AI solutions for procurement efficiency.
- Shufersal's operational procurement amounts to approximately 2.3 billion shekels annually.
- The current procurement process involves over 1,000 suppliers and is primarily manual.
- Issues with the existing procurement system include:
  - Inconsistent pricing and outdated price lists.
  - Lack of control over orders initiated by requesting units.
  - Difficulty in managing and updating price lists (maktim).
  - Long negotiation processes that can take months.
- The company is currently not using any advanced procurement technology.
- There is a strong emphasis on internal management and control rather than outsourcing procurement processes.
- AI could potentially help streamline the management of price lists and supplier negotiations.

- **Fit/not-fit hints**:
