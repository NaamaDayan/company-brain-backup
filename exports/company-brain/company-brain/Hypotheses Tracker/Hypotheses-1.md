H1: Vertical AI companies that replace BPO providers will scale faster than those replacing internal teams, due to clearer ROI and easier budget substitution.
H2: Mid-market companies with outsourced operations (not enterprise SaaS-heavy orgs) are the best early GTM segment for AI-native “managed services.”
H3: Procurement mid-market (without outsourcing) may be harder to monetize via headcount reduction, requiring alternative ROI framing (efficiency, spend optimization).
H4: “AI operations companies” that act as black-box workflow owners will eventually converge into a new category distinct from SaaS and traditional services.
H5: Industries with structured, repeatable workflows (insurance back-office, staffing ops, wholesale distribution) will show higher adoption than knowledge-heavy domains.
