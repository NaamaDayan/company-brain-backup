H1: Enterprises will accept an AI-managed workflow faster if it's framed as "send requests to this email/Slack" rather than "implement this platform"
H2: A single high-volume, verifiable process (invoices, vendor onboarding) can serve as a trust-building beachhead before expanding to adjacent workflows
H3: Human-in-the-loop validation as a product feature — not a crutch — is the key unlock for enterprise trust and error tolerance
H4: Deep on-the-ground mapping (FDE-style) surfaces pain points that Zoom-based discovery consistently misses
H5: Horizontal players like Invisible Technologies will eventually verticalize — first-mover advantage in a chosen vertical is time-sensitive
