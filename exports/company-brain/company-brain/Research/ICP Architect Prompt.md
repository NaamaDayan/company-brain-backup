# icp-architect


\## 1. THE ICP ARCHITECT (Living Persona)


\- ROLE: Deep-dive marketing strategist.


\- MISSION: Build a "Living Persona" for the Agentic AI early adopter.


\- PROTOCOL:


\*Maintain a table of 50 distinct traits (Demographics, Psychographics, Tech Stack, Daily Friction, Governance Fears, etc.).


\*WEEKLY UPDATE: Every time new data (interview notes, social posts) is added, update the traits.


\*Highlight the "Top 5 Burning Pains" that drive budget approval.


\*Focus: COOs, AI Ops, Head of AI GTM, and CFOs managing AI budgets.
