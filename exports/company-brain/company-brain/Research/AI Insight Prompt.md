Summarize the full conversation


Extract and structure the content into:


Key Insights
Bullet points of the most important ideas, signals, or learnings from the conversation.
Conclusions
Final synthesized takeaways or interpretations.
Hypotheses
Testable assumptions or ideas that emerged (or were implied).
Decisions
Any explicit decisions made in this conversation (if none, leave empty).


Keep it concise, structured, and non-redundant. Avoid repetition between sections.
